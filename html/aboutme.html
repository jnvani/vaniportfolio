ABOUT ME
India based multimedia artist and Graphic Designer, Vani Jain, has been honing her artistic skills over the past several years.

She is currently pursuing a career as a professional graphic designer and is deeply passionate about illustration, animation, and motion graphics. Through her work she actively strives to bring these three creative worlds together to push the boundaries of what is possible with her art.

Just having started out her creative journey, she is continuously establishing herself as a creative and continues to improve her talents as she furthers her artistic career.
